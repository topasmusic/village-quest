# Relics, Wayfinder, and Magic Shards

## Relic Overview

| Relic | Requirement | Main Effect |
|---|---|---|
| `Apiarist's Smoker` | `The Failing Harvest` + `200 Farming` | readies up to `10` hives per day and calms nearby bees |
| `Wayfinder's Compass` | `The Silent Forge` + `200 Crafting` | `Home`, later land bearings and roadmarks |
| `Merchant's Seal` | `Market Road Troubles` + `200 Village Trade` | rerolls pilgrim or wandering trader wares once per in-game day |
| `Shepherd's Flute` | `Restless Pens` + `200 Animals` | calls nearby animals for a short while |
| `Starreach Ring` | hidden shard trial | `+2` block reach in the offhand while building |

## Apiarist's Smoker

Unlock:
- complete `The Failing Harvest`
- reach `200 Farming Reputation`
- finish the `Apiarist's Smoker` special commission

Quest steps:
- harvest `8` honey bottles
- harvest `4` honeycomb cuts

Effect:
- up to `10` uses per in-game day
- readies a hive for harvest
- calms nearby bees

## Wayfinder's Compass

### Step 1: Get the compass

Requirements:
- complete `The Silent Forge`
- reach `200 Crafting Reputation`

Quest:
- mine `16` redstone ore
- craft `1` netherite pickaxe after accepting the quest
- turn in `1` plain unenchanted netherite pickaxe made for the quest

Reward:
- `Wayfinder's Compass`
- `3 Netherite Ingots`

Base mode:
- `Home`

### Home behavior

- normal right-click does not teleport immediately
- first right-click gives a confirmation warning
- second right-click within `10` seconds returns you home
- cooldown is roughly `30` minutes

### Step 2: Unlock biome reading through `Field Bearings`

Requirements:
- `Apiary Charter` built
- you are carrying the `Wayfinder's Compass`

Calibration task:
- inscribe `Desert`
- inscribe `Jungle`
- inscribe `Frozen Peaks`
- inscribe `Mushroom Fields`

How:
- stand in the target biome
- wait for the chat hint
- right-click the compass there

After completion, the compass unlocks the broader biome roster:

- `Forest`
- `Plains`
- `Taiga`
- `Snowy Plains`
- `Mountain`
- `Meadow`
- `Cherry Grove`
- `Jungle`
- `Desert`
- `Frozen Peaks`
- `Savanna`
- `Swamp`
- `Badlands`
- `Beach`
- `River`
- `Ocean`
- `Mushroom Fields`

### Step 3: Unlock structure reading through `Roadmarks for the Compass`

Requirements:
- `Market Charter` built
- `Wayfinder's Compass` already earned
- `Field Bearings` already completed
- structure modes not yet unlocked

This contract comes from the `Pilgrim`, not the `Questmaster`.

Calibration task:
- inscribe `Village`
- inscribe `Pillager Outpost`
- inscribe `Woodland Mansion`
- inscribe `Swamp Hut`

How:
- stand at the target structure
- wait for the chat hint
- right-click the compass there

After completion, the compass unlocks the broader structure roster:

- `Village`
- `Pillager Outpost`
- `Stronghold`
- `Trial Chamber`
- `Woodland Mansion`
- `Ocean Monument`
- `Jungle Temple`
- `Swamp Hut`
- `Mineshaft`
- `Shipwreck`
- `Ruined Portal`

### Step 4: Story-linked road modes during `Shadows on the Trade Road`

Once the late story `Shadows on the Trade Road` is active:

- chapter `2` sends you to a `Toolsmith` to calibrate the `Wayfinder's Compass`
- while a normal rescue signal is live, the mode list gains `Caravan Distress`
- during the final convoy chapter, the mode list switches to `Guild Convoy`
- these story modes are temporary live-target modes, not always-on permanent structure modes
- the signal can only bind to the encounter while that story step currently has an active target, usually at night

## Merchant's Seal

Unlock:
- complete `Market Road Troubles`
- reach `200 Village Trade Reputation`

Quest steps:
- complete `5` villager trades
- earn `8` emeralds from those deals
- make `1` purchase from a pilgrim stall

Effect:
- once per in-game day, sneak-use it on a `Pilgrim` or `Wandering Trader`
- rerolls wares
- on the `Pilgrim`, it may also reroll the current road rumor

## Shepherd's Flute

Unlock:
- complete `Restless Pens`
- reach `200 Animals Reputation`

Quest steps:
- breed `4` animals
- shear `3` sheep
- gather `8` wool

Effect:
- draws nearby animals toward you for a short while

## Magic Shards

### One-shard use

- after finishing the normal daily, `1 Magic Shard` can unlock one extra daily

### Ten-shard use

- carrying `10 Magic Shards` unlocks the hidden special path `Whispers of the Shards`

Trial tasks:
- obtain `4` amethyst clusters
- brew `3` potions
- enchant `1` item
- obtain `1` ender pearl
- obtain `1` blaze rod

Then:
- return to the `Questmaster`
- receive a buried-cache map
- dig up the cache
- recover the `Starreach Ring`

## Starreach Ring

Effect:
- when carried in the offhand, grants `+2` block reach while building
